Localizacion

Finca Miravalle es una finca para bodas y eventos situada entre los municipios de Collado Villalba y Guadarrama.

Está a 20 minutos en coche desde Las Rozas o media hora desde Madrid.

Dirección: Carretera de la Coruña, km 44. 28440 Guadarrama, Madrid.

Coordenadas: N40° 40' 21.72" W4° 5' 22.2"

Got it - but where can we stay? ↓

Accomodation

If you're planning on staying over, we recommend one of two options.

1. Oakwood Cottages, Darver

There is limited accommodation available for our guests in Oakwood Cottages located just outside the main castle entrance approximately stumbling distance from Darver Castle.

These cottages contain 12 double en-suite rooms & there is a lobby area with tea and coffee facilities.

Breakfast is served the next morning in the Grain-store Restaurant at Darver Castle.

One of the rooms is a large family room which will accomodate three adults.

To enquire about the Guest Cottages, please contact Darver Castle directly;

  • Phone: +353 (0)42 9379466
  • Email: oakwoodcottages@darvercastle.ie

2. The Crowne Plaza Hotel, Dundalk

We expect the majority of guests will stay in The Crowne Plaza Hotel in Dundalk, only 4 miles away.

The Crowne Plaza provides a shuttle bus to & from Darver Castle on the day of the wedding.

This will leave from outside The Crowne Plaza Hotel on the afternoon of the wedding at approximately 2.30pm sharp to ensure guests arrive in time for the ceremony starting at 3pm.

The bus will then be on hand from around midnight to make trips back over to the hotel as required.

If you are planning on staying in The Crowne Plaza - mention "McGee Wedding" when booking to avail of the special rate offered to our guests.

  • Phone: +353 (0) 42 939 4900
  • Email: enquiries@crowneplazadundalk.ie
